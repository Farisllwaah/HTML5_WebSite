SOCR Support and Donations

SOCR is a not-for-profit research and educational resource devoted to developing, validating and freely sharing knowledge, portable online aids for science education and tools for technology based instruction and scientific computing. SOCR efforts are partly supported by the US National Science Foundation and through the generosity of people like you, who believe that knowledge means power and that knowledge should be free. Please consider making donations of any size to support student stipends (for resource development and conference travel) and instructor-training/continuing-education workshops (travel and stipends for K-16 educators to attend annual SOCR events).

You can donate to SOCR in several ways:

  • Direct University of Michigan SOCR Project Giving Site,

  • Via the general University of Michigan online secure Giving Site - specify that the donation is marked for the Statistics Online Computational Resource (SOCR),

  • Via regular mail. Please make cheques payable to Regents of the University of Michigan. To prevent theft, please do not send cash through the mail. Our mailing address is:
